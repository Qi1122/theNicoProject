About our NICO product

Nico -- communicate and protect

Human need to communicate and detect danger in smog. Our product: NICO is to help people improving life quality.

Nico can communicate with another Nico carrier, and sense warning signal if someone is getting closer even the approaching person doesn’t wear Nico. A Nico set came with 3 parts:

Head Sensor: This can sense what we want to say from our brain, and pass the signal to the coat.

Expression Coat: It will show what we want to say with LED light, people will be able to express their emotion with different colors.

Decoding Glasses: It will decode messages from the other coat, and send decoded message to head sensor. Head sensor will transfer decoded messages to brain.
