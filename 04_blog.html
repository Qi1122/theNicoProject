This is a process image of how to implement the process. The idea of creating the object is using Andrino to send signal to LED strips. Andrino will also control how to visualize the sentences with different color.

Connect LED Stripes and Wires

There are 2 ways to connect LED with wires: using flux or connectors. Flux is helpful of soldering, and connectors to avoid soldering work. Flux it is really easier to connect the copper sheet and copper wire together, but it still needs a high soldering skill, or the LED strips cannot light. Connectors are really helpful at this point, combining them together would make this part successful.
