Our World

In 2049, the fire of California since 2018 still is burning, and spread to the whole world by the oil leaking. This is a Dystopia world, the world has been heavily polluted. Walking outside without any protection will be great harm of human health. Therefore, people have to wear masks to protect themselves, which would made communication inconvenient. Robbery became a serious society issue because people can only see each other within 5 feet.

Human need a new efficient way to communicate with each other urgently, and a way can help them detect danger from smog. Our design is a new way to help people with communication -- Communication Coat, and we name it Nico. Nico can communicate with another Nico carrier, and sense warning signal if someone is getting closer even the approaching person doesn’t wear Nico or doesn’t turn on Nico communication system.
